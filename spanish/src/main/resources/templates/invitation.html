You reported experiencing some anxiety. As a result, you are eligible to participate in the MindTrails study, a new Internet-based program designed to reduce anxious thinking.

This is an experimental program, so it is still in the testing phase, but there are many good reasons for you to participate.

Reasons to participate:
  • Prior research says it may reduce anxious thinking as effectively as therapy for some people.
  • It's FREE.
  • It will not require a lot of your time.
  • It can be completed anywhere — all you need is a computer, tablet, or smartphone with Internet access.
  • At the end of the study, we’ll give you feedback about how your anxiety symptoms have changed.
  • You can access a fun and interesting psychology demonstration after each session.

If you would like to participate, click the “Learn More” button below. If you do not wish to participate, consider reviewing these alternate Mental Health Resources for dealing with your anxiety.
